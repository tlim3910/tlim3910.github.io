A cylindrical vessel contains a large number of spherical beads made from some unknown material and a small float placed on top of them. An experimenter starts pouring water into the vessel. The water can freely pass between the beads. The plot shows the distance h between the float and the bottom of the vessel (in centimeters), as a function of volume V of water that was poured into the vessel (in liters). The total mass of all beads is m = 2.1 kg. What is the density of the beads material?
