A uniform coin is tossed up by an average force of 50 N applied for 0.08 seconds near its edge. Let's say we catch the coin when it falls down to the same level as it started so the side that is facing up at the moment can be forced to face up. If the coin has a mass of 30 g and is 2 cm side, can you guess if it would be heads or tails when caught? You may ignore the air resistance.
